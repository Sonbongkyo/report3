    Resource Sites:

    www.haruki-murakami.com — A fan site which features pictures, quotes, videos, artworks and news — “Everything related to the Japanese writer Haruki Murakami.”

    Murakami.ch is a complete resource, which includes links to articles, interviews, and essays about Murakami, as well as lively message boards.

    Translating Haruki Murkami is a blog that was started by Ika Kaminka and Anna Zielinska-Elliott, Murakami’s Norwegian and Polish translators. They post regularly about new editions, with interesting commentary on the challenges of translating Murakami’s work.
